## 🚀 Early Adopters Program

We're looking for organizations interested in being early adopters of V-COMM. Benefits include:

- **Direct input** on product roadmap and feature prioritization
- **Priority support** from the core development team
- **Early access** to new features and releases
- **Recognition** as a founding adopter
- **Discounted pricing** for enterprise features

### Requirements

- Active development or deployment of a communication platform
- Willingness to provide feedback and participate in user research
- Commitment to security best practices

### Apply

To join the Early Adopters Program:

1. Email us at [adopters@vantis.dev](mailto:adopters@vantis.dev)
2. Include your organization name, use case, and expected scale
3. Our team will review and respond within 48 hours
