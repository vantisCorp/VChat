## 🔄 Iteration Process

### Sprint Cycle
- **Duration**: 2 weeks
- **Planning**: Monday
- **Development**: Tuesday - Thursday
- **Testing**: Friday
- **Review**: Monday (next sprint)

### Release Cadence
- **Patch Releases**: Weekly (bug fixes)
- **Minor Releases**: Monthly (features)
- **Major Releases**: Quarterly (milestones)
